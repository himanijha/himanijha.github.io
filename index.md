I'm an undergraduate [Computer Science](https://www.cs.ucla.edu) student at [UCLA](https://www.ucla.edu) from the Bay Area.

I am interested in:

* **Systems and performance.** I'm a 2x software engineering intern at [Everpure](https://everpuredata.com/)'s Hyperscaler R&D business unit, supporting infrastructure for large-scale deployments. My work primarly covers the kernel side of their high-performance datapath, mostly dealing with [io_uring](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Io_uring), [KVM](https://linux-kvm.org/page/Main_Page), and [VFIO](https://docs.kernel.org/driver-api/vfio.html).

* **Accessibility & Human Interface.** I focus mostly on mobile, specifically iOS. [BinBot](https://github.com/himanijha/BinBot) is an iOS app I created that uses machine learning to help people sort their trash. I also made PianoTutor, an AR music environment built in Swift Playgrounds, which won [Apple's Swift Student Challenge](https://developer.apple.com/swift-student-challenge/). I co-developed [Folded Notes](https://github.com/himanijha/FoldedNotes), an anonymous peer support platform that uses haptic hardware. It was awarded 1st place and Most Accessible at [SWE@UCLA's hackathon](https://devpost.com/software/folded-notes).

* **Nonprofits and Social Impact.** Many nonprofits are budget constrained and unable to afford expensive business software, such as CRMs, internal dashboards, and other custom tools. I led development on the [Save Cantonese Volunteer Roster](https://github.com/himanijha/VolunteerRoster) to integrate with their communication platform. For [Alzheimer's San Diego](https://www.alzsd.org), I helped build an application to track and analyze their volunteer data. I also served as the developer lead on [Mending Kids](https://github.com/novaforgood/mending-kids), building a surgical supply inventory management system.
