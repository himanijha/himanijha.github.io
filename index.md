I'm an undergraduate Computer Science student at [UCLA](https://samueli.ucla.edu) from the Bay Area.


Here's what I am interested in:

* **Systems and performance.** I'm a 2x intern at [Everpure](https://everpuredata.com/) working on their high performance datapath for hyperscaler customers. My work has covered IPC, io_uring, hypervisors and virtual machines. 

* **Accessibility & Human Interface.** I focus mostly on mobile, specifically iOS. [BinBot](https://github.com/himanijha/BinBot/tree/main), is an iOS app I created that uses machine learning to help people sort their trash. I also made PianoTutor, an Augmented Reality application built in Swift Playgrounds, which won **[Apple's Swift Student Challenge](https://developer.apple.com/swift-student-challenge/)**. I co-developed [FoldedNotes](https://github.com/himanijha/FoldedNotes/tree/main), which is an anonymous peer support hotline that uses haptic hardware. It was awareded 1st place and Most Accessible at [SWE@UCLA's hackathon](https://devpost.com/software/folded-notes).

* **Nonprofits and Social Impact.** I led development on the [Save Cantonese Volunteer Roster](https://github.com/himanijha/VolunteerRoster) which flags inactive volunteers from Zoom and Slack logs. For [Alzheimer's San Diego](https://www.alzsd.org), I helped build a volunteer management system to track and analyze their volunteer data. I also served as dev lead on [Mending Kids](https://github.com/novaforgood/mending-kids), building an inventory system that helps keep their surgical supplies organized.
